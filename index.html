TEMPLE GYM - The world's famous gym in Birmingham

Our world famous and renowned Temple Gym, Birmingham, is located in the City Centre. Based in the Jewellery Quarter, our gym is a short 10-minute walk from Snow Hill Station, or a 15-minute walk from New Street Station.

On a number of bus routes, this gym in Birmingham is incredibly easy to get too and close to plenty of bars and restaurants, perfect for anyone who likes a drink or a snack after they train.

We provide all of our customers with FREE onsite parking.
